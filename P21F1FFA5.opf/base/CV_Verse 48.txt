And if the pramāṇas owe their establishment to the prameyas, and if those prameyas are to be
established by those very pramāṇas, how will the prameyas establish [the pramāṇas]?

And if the pramāṇas owe their establishment to the prameyas, and if those prameyas are to be
established by those very pramāṇas, [we encounter the following difficulty:] the pramāṇas
not having been established, the prameyas are not established, for their cause is not established.
How, then, will the prameyas establish the pramāṇas?
