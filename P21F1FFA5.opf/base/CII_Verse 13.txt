Just as ignorant people wrongly perceive a mirage as water, [and that wrong perception is removed
by some person who knows, in like manner you may think that] you negate a wrong perception
of a non-entity.

that perception, says: ‘But that mirage is without water’.Likewise, you may think that

perception of an intrinsic nature in things that are devoid of an intrinsic nature. To
