Because, if the prameyas owe their establishment to the pramāṇas, and if those pramāṇas are
to be established by those very prameyas, [we encounter the following difficulty:] the prameyas
not having been established, the pramāṇas are not established, for their cause is not
established. How, then, will the pramāṇas establish the prameyas?
